Our Philosophy

At Private Plates, we believe convenience should still feel exceptional. Whether we are stocking your fridge with beautifully prepared meals for the week or creating a more intimate private dining experience, our approach stays the same: food should feel fresh, personal, and held to a higher standard.

Our meal service is intentionally built around two deliveries each week. We take this approach because freshness matters. Instead of preparing food meant to sit for the entire week, we deliver in two rounds so meals maintain better texture, better flavor, and a more vibrant overall experience.

We believe personalization is just as important as quality. Every client has different tastes, routines, goals, and expectations, which is why our menus are tailored rather than templated. From nourishing weekly meal prep to elevated family-style dining and private chef experiences, each menu is created with care and adapted to the people it is made for.

We also believe the experience goes beyond the food itself. Our meals are packed in reusable glass containers for a cleaner, more refined alternative to plastic — practical for reheating, better for presentation, and more aligned with the level of care behind each delivery.

Rooted in professional kitchen experience and inspired by hospitality, Private Plates brings a chef-led approach to modern living: thoughtful food, polished execution, and a more elevated way to eat at home.
